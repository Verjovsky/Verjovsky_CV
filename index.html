Previous Experience:

Oncobiology Program

Rio de Janeiro - Brazil

From 2006 to 2019

The Oncobiology Program is part of Brazil’s National Cancer Foundation and supports the integration of cancer researchers from several centers from Rio de Janeiro city.

At the Communication Office, I provided them press advisory, maintained a bimonthly news report (Onconews), and administered the Program's website & social media.

I also developed communication researches about how cancer is framed by Brazilian media. You can check a list of my main publications below, along with my other academic achievements.

Acubens Virtual Cancer Museum

Online - Brazil

From 2012 to 2014

Acubens is a virtual museum developed by the Oncobiology Program. The website addresses cancer topics through games, videos and comic strips, focusing on risk factors and the promotion of a healthy lifestyle.

I contributed with concepts and contents of the virtual exhibitions.

The Federation of Societies for Experimental Biology (FeSBE)

Brazil

From 2007 to 2014, and 2019

The Fesbe's Annual Meetings are among the main biomedical conferences in Latin America.

I worked in the press office for nine editions of this event, being responsible for spotting trending topics in the program, interviewing scientists, and writing press releases about the conferences.

National Institute of structural biology and bioimaging (INBEB)

Rio de Janeiro - Brazil

From 2010 to 2014

INBEB is a Biological Imaging Center that hosts a high-technology and multi-user facility with nuclear magnetic resonance equipments, imaging by PET-SPECT-CT, bioluminescence, electron, atomic force and multiphoton microscopies, among others.

I was responsible for INBEB’s institutional communication, creating press releases to the media, as well as website & social media contents. I also organized symposiums and produced research reports to the founding institutions.

National Academy of Medicine (ANM)

Rio de Janeiro - Brazil

From 2012 to 2014

Founded in 1829, ANM dedicates to the development of Brazilian medicine and public health.

I collaborated with ANM’s communication office writing press releases for the press, as well as website and social media contents.

Brazilian Institute of Ophthalmology

Rio de Janeiro - Brazil

From 2013 to 2014

IBOL is Rio de Janeiro city’s first and main Eye Hospital.

I collaborated with IBOL’s communication office writing press releases, as well as website and social media contents.

The West Zone State University Center (UEZO)

Rio de Janeiro - Brazil

2010

UEZO is a public university center inaugurated in 2005 with the purpose of meeting the demand of students from the West Zone of Rio de Janeiro and expanding the technological and economic development of such regions.

For the period of six months, I was responsible for UEZO’s institutional communication, providing press advisory and creating content for their new webpage.

Science Today Institute

Rio de Janeiro - Brazil

2006

The Science Today Institute is a non profit organization that belongs to the Brazilian Society for the Advancement of Science. They maintain two popular science magazines, one for children and another aimed at a board public.

I collaborated with them for one year as a reporter, writing articles for both adults and children.
